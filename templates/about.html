What is a Covered Call?

A covered call is an options trading strategy where an investor holds a long position in a stock and sells (writes) call options on that same stock to generate additional income.

How it Works:

  1. Own the Stock: You must own at least 100 shares of the underlying stock
  2. Sell Call Options: Sell call options against your stock holdings
  3. Collect Premium: Receive premium income from selling the options
  4. Manage Risk: The stock you own "covers" the call option you sold

Benefits:

  • Generate additional income from stock holdings
  • Reduce portfolio volatility
  • Enhance returns in sideways or mildly bullish markets

Risks:

  • Limited upside potential if stock price rises significantly
  • Still exposed to downside risk of stock price decline
  • May need to sell stock if options are exercised

This App's Purpose

This application aims to use predictive models and data analysis to help identify the best opportunities for covered call strategies, optimizing risk-adjusted returns for your portfolio.
